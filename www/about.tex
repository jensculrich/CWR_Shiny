\subsection{Welcome !}\label{welcome}}

 This app will help you identify both the native range of CWRs in
Canada, and the extent of those species' ranges that are conserved ex
situ in botanic gardens of Canadian CWRs. Botanical gardens can provide
a unique role in both conserving CWRs while also educating the public on
the importance of CWRs through their outreach and public engagement
programs.

\href{https://github.com/jensculrich/CWR_project/blob/main/all_garden_accessions_with_geo_data.csv}{Canadian
CWR master-list}.

It has been made with \href{https://shiny.rstudio.com/}{shiny} and you
may find the code on
\href{https://github.com/jensculrich/CWR_Shiny}{github}.

For more information on Crop Wild Relatives look a the video below.
